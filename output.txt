Make Me A Video Game!

I the Gigantic and blue Timothy have painted Michael's running sister and plan to steal her colorful arrows!

What are a gorilla and backpacking dog to do? Before you can help Jane, you'll have to collect the funny books and bright shoes that open up the 7 worlds connected to A Jasmin Lair. There are 24 flowers and 10 swords in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
